## üí∞ Incentive Programs

### Summary
- **106 Total Incentive Programs**
- Programs cover multiple jurisdictions
- Include rebates, grants, and cost-sharing

### Major Programs Include:

#### 1. LA County Sanitation Districts Low-Water Rebate
- **Type:** Rebate
- **Status:** Active, ongoing
- **Coverage:** Reduced service charges for low-water users
- **Website:** [lacsd.org](https://www.lacsd.org/services/wastewater-programs-permits/wastewater-revenue-program/low-water-rebate-program)

#### 2. California SWEEP (State Water Efficiency and Enhancement Program)
- **Type:** Grant
- **Range:** $5,000 - $200,000 per farm
- **Annual Budget:** $85M
- **Period:** 2024-2026
- **Target:** Agricultural water conservation
- **Website:** [cdfa.ca.gov/oefi/sweep](https://www.cdfa.ca.gov/oefi/sweep)

#### 3. Save Our Water Conservation Rebate Program
- **Type:** Rebate
- **Range:** $50 - $3,000
- **Annual Budget:** $25M
- **Period:** 2022-2027
- **Coverage:** Greywater systems, rain barrels, turf replacement, irrigation
- **Eligibility:** All California residents
- **Website:** [saveourwater.com/rebates](https://saveourwater.com/en/rebates)

### Program Features Tracked:
- Incentive amounts (min/max)
- Annual and total budgets
- Application deadlines and cycles
- Eligible system types
- Income requirements
- Property requirements
- Installation requirements
- Stacking rules (combining programs)
- Contact information
